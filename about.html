Our Story

Learn more about our mission, values and the people behind Dominion Holdings.

Building Legacies Through Service

Dominion Holdings was founded by entrepreneurs who believe in the power of service‑based businesses. Our leadership team has over three decades of combined experience building and scaling companies across landscaping, home services, janitorial and other “boring” but essential industries. We know what it takes to create lasting value—and we’re committed to preserving the legacy you’ve built while unlocking new opportunities for growth.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We honour our word and do the right thing, even when no one is watching.
  • People First: We invest in teams, not just balance sheets, because businesses thrive when people do.
  • Long‑Term Vision: We think decades—not quarters—and measure success by enduring impact.
  • Transparency: We communicate openly and honestly throughout the acquisition process and beyond.
