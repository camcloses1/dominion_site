About Our Founder & Mission

Dominion Holdings was founded by Cam Reedy, a United States Air Force veteran who has dedicated his life to service. After years of leading teams in high‑stakes environments, Cam brings the same level of integrity, discipline and care to acquiring and scaling small businesses. He knows that behind every company are real people and a legacy worth protecting.

Our mission is to preserve legacy and scale it. We’re watching Wall Street take over Main Street across the country, often stripping away the culture and values that made those companies special. Not here. At Dominion Holdings we aim to be phenomenal stewards—protecting the business itself, the legacy you’ve built and the people who work there. We’re scrappy, innovative and determined to help owners achieve a successful exit with peace of mind. Protecting then scaling means applying modern technology and innovative solutions into the business for enhanced operations and maximum efficiency.
