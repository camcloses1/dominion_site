Our Approach

We look beyond numbers to find great service‑based businesses with heart and potential.

Investment Philosophy

At Dominion Holdings, we invest in small to mid‑sized service businesses that are profitable, resilient and loved by their customers. We believe in patient capital and long‑term stewardship. Our approach is not about flipping companies for a quick profit but rather partnering with owners to expand what they’ve built.

Acquisition Criteria

  • Service‑based companies in industries like landscaping, home maintenance, cleaning and related sectors
  • Stable revenues with loyal customer base
  • Strong management or key team members that can stay on post‑acquisition
  • Demonstrated profitability and growth potential
